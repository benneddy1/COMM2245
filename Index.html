NVIDIA

INTRODUCTION

NVidia is an American technology company that was founded on April 5, 1993 in Sunnyvale, California. NVidia specializes in the production and innovation of GPUs as producing the most advanced chips, systems, and software for advancements in Artificial Intelligence. NVidia is mainly known for GPUs and they have many are apparent in many industries that range from supercomputers, edge-to-cloud computing, and workstations such as manufacturing, engineering, etc.

WHAT ARE THERE STRATEGIES

Nvidia's Marketing strategies are focused on understanding the customer's demands, aligning their products and services to them. They use strategies to market their products in a variety of ways such as content creation, influencer marketing, and advertising through social media. Their powerful Graphing Processing Units (GPUs) are what makes the company what it is today. They offer a wide range of GPUs to data scientists, gamers, and companies for top-of-the-line hardware.
